Target Audience

Target audience will be the general public that has access to the internet using a mobile phone, tablet, or desktop.

  • Government: Comprised of national and local government agencies responsible for the safety and security of the people under their jurisdiction.
  • Food Industry: Farmers, livestock and fish raiser, and other institutions related to food production.
  • Private Sector: Businesses owned by private individuals or groups.
  • Local Community: Group of interacting people living in a common location, organized around common values and is attributed with social cohesion, generally in social units larger than a household.

Scenarios

  • How is the weather today?
  • How will the weather today affect me and my business?
  • Will the weather condition this coming week permit me to harvest my crops?
  • Where can I find information on weather condition about my place?
  • What is the weather forcast for a month?
