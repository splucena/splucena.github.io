Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform, educate, and update the citizen of the Philippines on current and future weather condition. It aims to dissiminate weather updates as it happen and where it happens. It is targeted towards the general audience who have access to an internet or data capable mobile, tablet, or desktops.
